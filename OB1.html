Meaning, Characteristics & Determinants

📖 PERSONALITY DEFINED
PERSONALITY
Key Definitions
Allport (1937)
Dynamic psychophysical organization determining unique adjustments. Integrated system of traits.
Robbins
Sum of reactions and interactions. Observable patterns of behavior.
Cattell
Predictor of behavior in situations. Measurable psychological construct.
🧬 DETERMINANTS
Biological Factors
Genetics
Chromosomes, inherited traits. Twin studies show 40-50% heritability of personality.
Brain Structure & Hormones
Neurotransmitters, limbic system influence temperament and emotional responses.
Environmental & Situational
Family & Culture
Parenting styles, cultural values, early experiences shape personality development.
Situation & Context
Strong situations constrain behavior; weak situations allow personality expression.

Major Personality Theories

📊 THEORETICAL PERSPECTIVES
THEORIES
Trait Theory
Big Five (OCEAN)
Openness, Conscientiousness, Extraversion, Agreeableness, Neuroticism. Most researched.
Cattell 16 Factors
More detailed trait assessment. Forms basis of 16PF instrument.
Psychodynamic & Behavioral
Freud's Theory
Id/Ego/Superego. Defence mechanisms. Unconscious motivations.
Bandura's Social Learning
Reciprocal determinism. Self-efficacy. Observable behavior shaped by environment.
Humanistic Approach
Rogers & Maslow
Self-concept, congruence, self-actualisation. Holistic human potential.

OB Attributes & Organizational Impact

🎯 KEY OB ATTRIBUTES
OB TRAITS
Important Personality Dimensions
Locus of Control
Internal = self-determined; External = fate-driven
Machiavellianism
Pragmatic manipulation for goals. High = cunning; Low = guileless
Type A vs B
A = competitive, impatient; B = relaxed, patient
Impact on Work Behavior
Job Performance
Conscientiousness predicts task performance; Agreeableness impacts teamwork.
Leadership
Extraversion, dominance predict leader emergence and effectiveness.
Creativity & Innovation
Openness to experience strongest correlate with innovative output.
